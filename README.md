# PowerBi_Ecommerce_Sales_Analysis

1.Project Title: Ecommerce Sales Dashboard | Power BI Business Intelligence Project

2.Description: The Ecommerce Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze and visualize online sales performance across states, product categories, customers, and payment modes. This dashboard provides key business insights into Sales Amount, Profit, Quantity Sold, and Customer purchasing behavior, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions and understand ecommerce trends.

3.Tech Stacks: • Power BI Desktop 📊 • DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) 🧠 • Power Query (Data Transformation) 📂 • Data Modeling 📈 • Interactive Data Visualization 📊

4.Data Source: The dataset was sourced from publicly available ecommerce sales data (commonly used Power BI practice dataset for analytics and dashboard development).

5.Feature / Highlights:
1️⃣ Sales Analysis • Total Sales Amount performance • Sales distribution by State • Customer-wise sales analysis • Category-wise quantity analysis
2️⃣ Profit Analysis • Monthly profit trend analysis • Profit comparison across product sub-categories • Identify high-performing products and profitable segments
3️⃣ Payment Insights • Sales quantity distribution by payment modes (COD, UPI, EMI, Debit Card, Credit Card) • Understand customer payment preferences
4️⃣ Product Insights • Category-wise product demand (Clothing, Electronics, Furniture) • Sub-category profit performance (Printers, Phones, Bookcases, Accessories, Stole)
5️⃣ Interactive Filters • Quarter filters (Qtr1, Qtr2, Qtr3, Qtr4) • Category filter dropdown • Dynamic visuals responding to user selections
6️⃣ Business Objective • Analyze ecommerce sales and profit performance • Identify top-performing states and customers • Understand product demand across categories • Monitor monthly profit trends and customer purchasing behavior
